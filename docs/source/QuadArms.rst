Quad Arms
----------
3D parts required,
cut and file CF to length XXmm
slide on parts the right way,
drill 3x motor holes at 90 degrees to the top of the mount,
Slide XXmm heatshrink over CF bar and slide the ESC through the heashrink to the middle.
Screw on motors using correct length screws to avoid motor coils and loctite
Position middle 3D prints to the center
Make the cable taught and heatshrink with cables on the leading edge of the CF bar if possible
